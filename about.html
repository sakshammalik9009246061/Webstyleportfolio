About Hi, I'm Saksham Malik

A passionate BCA student & aspiring Web Developer.

I’m Saksham Malik, a BCA student who loves creating modern and responsive websites. I am passionate about front-end development and I enjoy building clean UI designs with smooth animations and user-friendly layouts.

How I Got Here

My journey started with curiosity about how websites work. I began learning HTML and CSS, and soon realized how exciting it is to build something creative using code. After that, I started learning JavaScript to make websites more interactive and dynamic.

Currently, I am pursuing BCA and improving my skills daily by building projects and practicing coding regularly. I love exploring new web technologies and learning how professional websites are developed.

My Values & Beliefs

Consistency

I strongly believe that consistency is more important than talent. Practicing daily, even for a short time, helps build strong skills over time.

Discipline

Discipline is the key to success. I always try to stay focused, complete my work on time, and keep improving myself as a developer.

Learning Mindset

Technology changes quickly, so learning never stops. I love learning new tools and technologies to stay updated in the IT field.

Smart Work

I believe in working smartly with proper planning and clear goals. Smart work makes projects more effective and improves productivity.

Why Work With Me

I focus on creating websites that look modern, professional, and work smoothly on all devices. I can build responsive layouts, clean UI designs, and interactive elements using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

I take every project seriously and always try to deliver the best result possible. My goal is to create websites that provide value and help people or businesses grow online.

A Few More Words About Myself

Apart from web development, I also like exploring new technologies and improving my communication skills. I enjoy working on creative ideas and building projects that improve my portfolio.

My future goal is to become a full-stack developer and work in a good IT company where I can learn, grow, and contribute to real-world projects.
